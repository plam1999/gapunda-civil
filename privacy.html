Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Gapunda Civil Construction ("Gapunda", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal information when you visit our website at gapundacivil.com.au or contact us.

This policy is consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) set out in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit an enquiry through our contact form (name, email address, phone number, service interest, message)
  • Contact us directly via email
  • Engage with our services or projects

We do not knowingly collect sensitive information (such as health, race, or religious beliefs).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide quotes
  • Provide and manage our civil construction services
  • Communicate with you about projects, services, or business matters
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers who help us operate our business (such as email delivery services)
  • Contractors, clients, or partners directly involved in a project you have enquired about
  • Government or regulatory bodies where required by law

5. Form Submissions

Our contact form uses Web3Forms (web3forms.com) to deliver submissions to our email. By submitting the form, your information is transmitted through Web3Forms' servers. Please refer to their privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

6. Cookies & Analytics

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics services at this time. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notice.

7. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. However, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or out-of-date information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for certain uses of your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@gapundacivil.com.au.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with the updated date at the top.
