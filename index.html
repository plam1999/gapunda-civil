Who We Are

About Gapunda

Gapunda Civil Construction is a proudly Indigenous-owned civil construction company based in Darwin, Northern Territory. Gapunda delivers major and minor construction services across the high-rise, industrial, and civil sectors.

With extensive experience working alongside major Tier 1 and Tier 2 contractors, Gapunda brings professionalism, quality workmanship, and a commitment to safety on every project.

Gapunda is committed to local employment, Indigenous participation, and building lasting relationships with our clients and communities across the NT.
